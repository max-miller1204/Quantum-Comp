2. Draw a Bloch sphere and label the following locations.

(b) Where a qubit is |+) and |—).
(c) Where a qubit is |+ i) and |— i).

3. (a) State the three-part mathematical definition of a density operator, and explain each part of the
definition.

(c) Write down an example of a pure quantum state for one qubit.
